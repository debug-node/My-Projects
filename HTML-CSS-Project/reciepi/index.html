Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix all the ingredients into a smooth dough.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Soak in sugar syrup and serve warm.

Barfi Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Prepare dough with khoya and flour.
  2. Make small rolls and fry them.
  3. Dip them in sugar syrup and top with barfi.
  4. Garnish with dry fruits and serve chilled.
